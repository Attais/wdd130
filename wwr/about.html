Splash Co

At Splash Co, the river is our playground, and adventure is our creed. Join us as we navigate the currents of life with courage, camaraderie, and an unbridled passion for the wild. Together, let's make waves and leave a legacy that flows on forever.

History

Established in 1995, Splash Co began as a small, family-owned white water rafting company nestled in the heart of the Appalachian Mountains. Founded by avid outdoorsman John Smith, the company quickly gained renown for its thrilling rafting adventures and commitment to safety. Over the years, Splash Co expanded its operations, adding new river routes and outdoor activities to cater to adventurers of all ages and experience levels. Today, Splash Co stands as a premier destination for white water enthusiasts, offering unforgettable experiences that blend adrenaline-pumping thrills with the serenity of nature. With a legacy built on passion, expertise, and a love for the great outdoors, Splash Co continues to inspire adventure seekers from around the world to embark on unforgettable journeys down the river's wild currents.
