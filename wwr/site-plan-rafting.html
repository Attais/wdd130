Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Spash Co is to help those looking to get into white water rafting navigate the rocks they'll find along the way.

Audience

Do you like living life on the edge? Do you like to build teamwork and upper body strength? Do you want to have an excuse to see your friends fall into a river? Then Splash Co will help you get there!
